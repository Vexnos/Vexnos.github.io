Imaginary Biscuit League

Imaginary Biscuits are the currency of the future!

Benefits include:

  • Bribe your friends!
  • Confuse people!
  • Exploit the innocent!

How do I learn this power?:

  1. Sign up to the Imaginary Biscuit League, you can do that here
  2. Get into a situation where an individual has the economic upper hand
  3. Bribe them with an imaginary biscuit
  4. Profit!
  5. Tell them about the Imaginary Biscuit League
